How to sort the news


if you have many news maybe you can find useful the "Sort by" functions; they will order the news in the news list as you select in the menu



the available sort functions are:
  • Downloaded: the news are sorted in the same way as they are stored in the database, so as they have been downloaded
  • Date: the news are ordered by date/time of publishing regardless the date/time of download
  • Title: the alphabetical order
  • Unread: sort the news in 2 categories, read and unread
  • Author: if the news have an Author, the news are put in order by Authors name
the Downloaded type of sort is the default
