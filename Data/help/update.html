How to download the news


to immediately download the news you can choose either one of these 2 methods:
  1. Click on menu News and select Download News
  2. Click on "↓" button
both methods will start the same action, the statusbar above will show you the progress of the download process. You can immediately start this action by using the Command+o shortcut.

If you want to keep your alligator in your backyard, you can allow it to get its own food.
There's a way to enable a timer and automatically start the download process. you can do it by opening File -> Settings dialog and looking for Timer tab, there you'll find the "enabled" checkbox and the  "timer" value
expressed in microseconds.

Note: once a news is downloaded this will not be updated if it's updated upstream (a little shortcoming, maybe a future version fix?)
