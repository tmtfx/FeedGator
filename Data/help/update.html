How to download the news


to download the news you can choose one of these 2 methods:
  1. Click on menu File and select Get News
  2. Click on "Get" button
both methods will start the same action, a progress bar will appear in the left side and over the news list a string will inform you how many new news have been downloaded.

By selecting a newspaper you can decide to download only the selected one, if you want to download all the newspapers you have to select All in the newspaper list
