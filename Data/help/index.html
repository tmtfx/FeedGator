A simple Feed Aggregator

Jul 2, 2011 by Fabio Tomat aka TmTFx

This piece of code is a simple Feed Aggregator for Haiku and BeOS written in python.
I hope you enjoy it even if it has a lot of limits.
The program stores in a database the feeds of your favorite newspapers and at your command it downloads the latest news; if available it can display a little summary of the news and if you want you can open the news in your web browser.
