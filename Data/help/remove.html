How to remove feeds


to remove a feed you can choose one of these 2 methods:
  1. Click on menu File and select Remove Feed
  2. Click on "-" button
both methods open the same button under the newspaper list and the program will change like this:




so, after selecting the newspaper you wanna remove press the Remove button and then a window will ask you what to do with the relative news, you can delete them or keep them but they will be available only by selecting "All" in the newspaper list
